First introduced in 1993 by Richard Garfield under company name Wizards of the Coast, Magic: The Gathering has produced millions of collectible playing cards, over various themed sets throughout the years. Some of these cards more sought after than others...

History

Magic: The Gathering is well known for its strong value as a collector. The most collectible and highly sought after cards come from it's earliest set releases; known as the Alpha and Beta edition set blocks.

Vintage Sets

From the first set, Alpha edition, comes the highly sought after Black Lotus card. Black Lotus is part of a group of 9 cards dubbed the 'Power 9', being the most powerful (and collectible) cards ever printed in the franchise.
