Alpha booster boxes were first introduced in 1993 as a start for both players and collectors alike. Finding a sealed Alpha box today is near impossible; single cards from this set can go for tens of thousands of dollars. Below is a table of some of the vintage sets, the year they were produced, and rough estimate of the value of a sealed set today.
